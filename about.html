Our Story

Building a community of excellence since 2012

Academic Excellence

Established in 2012, the University of Energy and Natural Resources (UENR) has been committed to providing quality education in energy and natural resources. Our hostel system was developed to ensure students have access to comfortable, affordable accommodation during their academic journey.

Quality Accommodation

With three main hostels - University Hall 1, University Hall 2 (on-campus), and Credit Union Hostel (off-campus) - we provide flexible accommodation options to meet the diverse needs of our student population.

Safety & Security

All our hostels meet strict safety standards with 24/7 security monitoring, ensuring a safe and secure environment for all residents.
