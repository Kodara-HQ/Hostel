About University Hall 1

Located on the main campus of the University of Energy and Natural Resources, University Hall 1 offers comfortable on-campus accommodation with 4-person rooms. Perfect for students who want to be close to academic facilities while enjoying a supportive learning environment.

Our hostel features modern study areas, shared bathrooms, and excellent facilities. We pride ourselves on creating a conducive environment where students can focus on their academic pursuits while building lasting friendships with fellow students.
